Design Excellence

Our innovative design studio is the heart of our creative process. Here, our team of skilled designers pioneers new products, treatments, and techniques. We merge traditional craftsmanship like hand padding, hand sewing, knife-cutting, and dip-dyeing with cutting-edge methods such as laser cutting and embossing to craft a versatile product range.

In addition to our seasonal offerings, we collaborate closely with clients to develop bespoke merchandise tailored to their unique requirements. Our in-house seasonal and client-specific collections take center stage in our showrooms.
